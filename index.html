    Quick Tutorial

    Play normal international chess on a calculus-themed board. White and Black each begin with 20 minutes.

    • After each player makes three legal moves, that player answers an AP Calculus AB multiple-choice question.
    • A correct answer adds five minutes to that player’s clock.
    • A wrong answer pauses the game until that player sacrifices any own non-king piece.
    • Recall takes back the latest move; against the computer it recalls the full human-plus-bot turn.
    • Player vs Computer uses a local browser-side engine with adjustable ELO.
